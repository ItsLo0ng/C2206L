NOUNS

A noun represent a person, place, animal, thing or an idea

KINDS OF NOUN

Noun are classified as follows:
Proper: It is the name of a particular object, whether person, place, animal, idea or thing.
Common: It is the name possessed by things in general.
Collective: It is the name representing a group of things. Abstract: It is the name representing qualities, emotions, conditions of actions

PRONOUNS

A pronoun is a word used in place of a noun. A pronoun is generally used to avoid repetition of a noun.
